## Content Formulas

### The AIDA Framework

**Attention:** Hook them with something surprising or relevant
**Interest:** Make them want to know more
**Desire:** Show them the benefit
**Action:** Tell them what to do next

---

### The PAS Framework

**Problem:** Identify the pain point
**Agitate:** Make them feel the problem
**Solution:** Present your answer

---

### The Hook Types

**Curiosity gap:**
"I made [unexpected result] by doing [counterintuitive thing]..."

**Contrarian:**
"Stop [thing everyone does]. Here's why..."

**Number hook:**
"[X] things I learned from [experience]..."

**Story hook:**
"Last year I [failure/challenge]. Today I [success]..."

**Direct value:**
"How to [achieve desired outcome] in [timeframe]..."

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Twitter/X

- No external links in tweets (kills reach)
- Put links in replies or bio
- Threads outperform singles for reach
- Early engagement (first 30 min) matters most
- Visuals increase engagement 2-3x
- Quote tweets > regular retweets

### LinkedIn

- First line must hook (it's what shows in feed)
- No links in post body (put in comments)
- Line breaks for readability
- End with a question
- Personal beats corporate 10x
- Carousels/documents get high engagement

### General

- Native content > cross-posting
- Consistency > virality
- Engage with replies
- One CTA per post
- Test and iterate

## Engagement Boosters

### Questions That Work

- "What's your experience with [topic]?"
- "Agree or disagree?"
- "What would you add?"
- "What's the biggest challenge you face with [topic]?"
- "Has anyone else experienced this?"

### Call-to-Actions

- "Follow for more [topic]"
- "RT to share with your network"
- "Drop [emoji/word] if you want [resource]"
- "Tag someone who needs to see this"
- "Link in comments"

## Content Calendar Framework

### Weekly Rhythm (Twitter)

- **Monday:** Motivation/goals for the week
- **Tuesday:** Tactical tip or how-to
- **Wednesday:** Thread or longer content
- **Thursday:** Behind the scenes / building in public
- **Friday:** Wins, lessons, or reflection
- **Weekend:** Personal or engagement-focused

### Weekly Rhythm (LinkedIn)

- **Monday:** Industry insight or trend
- **Tuesday:** Tactical how-to
- **Wednesday:** Personal story or lesson
- **Thursday:** Contrarian take or opinion
- **Friday:** Roundup, reflection, or question

## Hashtag Guidelines

### Twitter/X
- 1-2 relevant hashtags max
- Often better to skip them entirely
- Only use if actively searched

### LinkedIn
- 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Mix of broad (#marketing) and specific (#b2bsaas)
- Place at end of post

### General Rule
- Hashtags for discovery, not decoration
- Research which are actually searched
- Fewer is usually better
